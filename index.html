Describing the UART protocol

06-04-2020
#UART 
UART (Universal Asynchronous Receiver Transmitter ) This protocol is asynchronous in the sense that the clocks between the 2 communicating devices are not in sync. So how is the communication achieved? This is done by using the real-time as an agreement for the transfers. What I mean with this is that even if one device has a clock of 100MHz and the other of 500MHz then they agree that in specific time slots there will be new data.
