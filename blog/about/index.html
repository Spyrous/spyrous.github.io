About

My name is Spyros “Spyrous” Chiotakis. Like you, i’m just a regular person trying to find my path through life. Currently I am a student at Technological Educational Institute of Crete *(Informatics Engineering Department)* and on course of getting my bachelor’s degree.

This blogs purpose is to educate on topics such as, compilers, algorithms, OS operations (and other similar ones) so that one can simply understand the functions behind the “black box” that was meant to change our lives forever. Most of the examples will be in C/C++ but I hope (if my time allows it) to illustrate a broader range of languages written so we will be able to figure out the power of every programming paradigm.

Here’s a mashup of the topics that interest me: Digital Design, Computer Architecture, Compilers, Operating Systems and generally computer infrastructure.
